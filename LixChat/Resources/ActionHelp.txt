Username aliases:
	case insensitive, * means any user can trigger the Action.
Keywords for Action:
	&rd[minimum,maximum] = generates a random Number.
	&rd[FileName] = takes a random Line from \"Filename\".
	...File must be in Script Folder.(can be found in Settings)
	&name = replace with caller name. (who has triggered the Action)
	&channel = replace with channel name.
	&word[index] = replace with word, at index, from calling Message.
